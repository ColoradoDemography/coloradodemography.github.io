Welcome to the Colorado Census 2020 Main Page

The 2020 Census will be administered beginning in April, 2020.

This web page provides information about the Census along with resources communities and constituent groups can use to achieve a complete count of people in Colorado.

The contents of this page will develop as we get closer to Census Day, April 1 2020.

This site is maintained by the State Demography Office. Be sure to visit this site often. NEW STUFF

Why Complete the Census?

Completing the Census provides multiple benefits for the State of Colorado and your community. It also gives you the chance to show the world that you are here.

Census Complete Count Campaign

The Colorado Complete Count Campaign (CCC) is a volunteer committee established to increase awareness about Census 2020 and to motivate residents in the community to respond with the result of the best, most accurate, count in Colorado.
