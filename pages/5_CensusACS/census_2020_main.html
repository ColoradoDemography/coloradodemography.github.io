Welcome to the Colorado Census 2020 Homepage

Census Day is April 1, 2020.

This website provides information about the Census along with resources communities and constituent groups can use to achieve a complete count of people in Colorado.

The contents of this page will develop as we get closer to Census Day, April 1, 2020.

This site is maintained by the State Demography Office. Be sure to visit this site often.

Why Complete the Census?

Completing the Census provides multiple benefits for the State of Colorado and your community. It also gives you the chance to show the world that you are here.

State Complete Count Campaign

The Colorado Complete Count Campaign (CCC) is a volunteer committee established to increase awareness about Census 2020 and to motivate residents in the community to respond with the result of the best, most accurate, count in Colorado.

Census Data Tools

  • The Census Response Tracker: An online tool for tracking Census form submissions. Data will be available beginning April, 2020.
  • Census Response Outreach Area Mapper: The Response Outreach Area Mapper (ROAM) is a tool developed by the U.S. Census Bureau to identify hard-to-survey areas.
