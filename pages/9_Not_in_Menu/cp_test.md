## Colorado Demographic Profiles

- - -

Demographic information is critical for making informed decisions at the local, state and national level. This demographic profile is a tool to present the summary of trends in a community. The dashboard provides charts, text, data and additional links to assist in the exploration and understanding of demographic trends for counties and municipalities in Colorado. The collection of tables and charts establishes the context for assessing potential impacts and for decision-making.

### To generate a profile for your county or municipality start [here](https://gis.dola.colorado.gov/apps/ProfileDashboard2/)
